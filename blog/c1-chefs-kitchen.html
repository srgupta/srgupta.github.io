Version Control:

The Big Picture

December, 2014

Before trying to understand what version control is, I found it helpful to understand why we need it; and, I began to understand why after realizing that Version Control Systems can be - and are - used for almost anything, not just software development. Thinking about the familiar case of writing a short essay, for example, you probably begin the paper with a sentence, then add another, and another, etc. Along the way, you might delete, modify, or add stuff, i.e. make changes.
